NHN Timeline

  • 2008
    Established Nyaya Health Nepal
    A mission-driven organization committed to improving healthcare delivery in rural Nepal.
  • 2014
    Launched Digital Health Program
    Leveraging technology to enhance healthcare access and coordination.
  • 2015
    Introduced Community Health Program
    Empowered local communities through grassroots healthcare initiatives.
  • 2016
    Began Managing Charikot Hospital
    Took leadership in strengthening hospital operations and care delivery in Dolakha.
  • 2017
    Started Services at Chaurpati Hospital
    Expanded healthcare services in remote areas of Achham.
  • 2019
    Played a Major Role in the Fight Against COVID-19
    Mobilized frontline services and digital tools to support pandemic response.
