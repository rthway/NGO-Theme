ABOUT US

Nyaya Health Nepal

Nyaya Health Nepal (NHN) is a non-profit organization dedicated to strengthening healthcare access for underserved communities of rural Nepal since 2008. Collaborating closely with the Ministry of Health and Population, Provincial Governments, and Municipal Authorities, NHN focuses on building integrated care delivery systems by providing a free care services to the underserved populations in line with Article 35 of the Constitution of Nepal and SDG Goal 3 as an effective model for rural healthcare. Operating in Achham District, Far West Nepal, NHN manages Bayalpata Hospital, a Municipal Primary Health Center (PHC) at Chaurpati rural municipality. These efforts are supported by NHN’s Community Health program and Electronic Health Record (EHR) system, ensuring integration of hospital-based and community health services.
